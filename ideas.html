My Ideas

These are my ideas to be implemented at school.

Idea Description
Screens for kids to see who is leaving There will be screens in certain areas of the school where kids can see if they are leaving.
Automatic sprinklers Sprinklers that turn on when it is too hot and turn off when they are not needed.
Digital Attendance System A system where students can check in and out of school.
Digital boards instead of whiteboards Instead of whiteboards, there will be digital boards for easier use.
Digital suggestion box A box where students can suggest ideas to be implemented.
School event tracker A tracker where students can see what events are happening.
Cafeteria screens Instead of manually changing the menu, digital screens makes it easier to manipulate.
